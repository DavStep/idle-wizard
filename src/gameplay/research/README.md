# Research

Research is the catalog of studies shown in the Research room.

Current scope uses prices and mana-upgrade formulas from `research-balance.json`. Buying a study spends gold and records completion. Sage Seed research is free and unlocks seed summoning; other seed unlock studies add their seed to the summon drop pool in catalog order. Recipe unlock studies are also catalog-ordered: each potion requires the previous potion research first. Mana production and cap research apply immediately. Summon multipliers and potion brewing permissions are still inactive.
