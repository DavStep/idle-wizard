# Research

Research is the catalog of studies shown in the Research room.

Current scope uses SpacetimeDB `research_config` for regular gold overrides and `game_config.research` for baseline gold/crystal costs. Regular studies spend gold; advanced automation studies spend crystal. Buying a study records completion. Sage Seed research is free and unlocks seed summoning; other seed unlock studies add their seed to the summon drop pool in catalog order. Summon multiplier studies are ordered x2, x3, x4, then x5. Recipe unlock studies are also catalog-ordered: each potion requires the previous potion research first. Mana production and cap growth come from player level, not research.
