# Research

Research is the catalog of studies shown in the Research room.

Current scope uses SpacetimeDB `research_config` for regular coin overrides and `game_config.research` for baseline coin/crystal/ruby/emerald costs. Regular studies spend coin; automation studies spend ruby; advanced friction/capacity/automation/research-efficiency/room studies spend emerald; crystal plot/cauldron entries are presented as level-ups, not studies. Crystal plot/cauldron level-up prices depend only on the upgrade level: first level-up costs 2 crystal, second costs 4, independent of plot or cauldron number. Cauldron stars start at 0 by default; the first 2-crystal cauldron level-up shows 1 star while keeping the internal brew multiplier at 2. Buying a study or level-up records completion. sage seed research is free and unlocks seed summoning; other seed unlock studies add their seed to the summon drop pool in catalog order. mana tonic recipe research is the free first potion unlock in the tutorial flow. Seed/herb and recipe unlock studies also have player-level gates in the research definition balance. Summon multiplier studies are ordered x2, x3, x4, then x5. Recipe unlock studies are catalog-ordered: each potion requires the previous potion research first. Stall staffing is a five-stage emerald lane, one study per trader stand; each completed stage makes that stand sell two items per five-second cycle. Legacy fast-sell research ids migrate to stages 1–3. Research time reduction is an 8-stage emerald study that reduces future research timers by 10% per completed stage, up to 80%. Research cost reduction is an 8-stage emerald study that reduces future coin research costs by 10% per completed stage, up to 80%. Automation reserve research is a 3-stage emerald lane gated by Prestige 4; it refines auto summon reserve presets and step size. Crystal, ruby, and emerald researches complete quickly; seed and recipe unlocks use the longer content-unlock timer curve. Mana production and cap growth come from player level, not research.

Capacity researches are advanced emerald studies. `plot 6-12 capacity` requires 1-7 completed prestiges, and `cauldron 3-5 capacity` requires 1-3 completed prestiges. Completed capacity research is permanent across prestige resets, permanently spends emerald, and makes that capacity buyable immediately in the run without waiting for the level cap. Slot-specific automation, speed, and crystal researches use the higher of the player-level cap and completed capacity research, so prestige slots get their matching rows. Speed research still resets with the run.

Timed researches can run concurrently without a slot limit. Prestige 5 gates advanced plot growth and cauldron brewing levels 6-10.
