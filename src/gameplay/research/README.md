# Research

Research is the catalog of studies shown in the Research room.

Current scope uses SpacetimeDB `research_config` for regular coin overrides and `game_config.research` for baseline coin/crystal/ruby costs. Regular studies spend coin; automation studies spend crystal; advanced studies spend ruby. Buying a study records completion. sage seed research is free and unlocks seed summoning; other seed unlock studies add their seed to the summon drop pool in catalog order. mana tonic recipe research is the free first potion unlock in the tutorial flow. Seed/herb and recipe unlock studies also have player-level gates in the research definition balance. Summon multiplier studies are ordered x2, x3, x4, then x5. Recipe unlock studies are catalog-ordered: each potion requires the previous potion research first. Fast sell payout research raises manual NPC sale payout from 80% to 85%, 90%, then 95%. Mana production and cap growth come from player level, not research.

Capacity researches are advanced ruby studies. `plot 11-20 capacity` requires 1-10 completed prestiges, and `cauldron 6-10 capacity` requires 2, 4, 6, 8, and 10 completed prestiges. Completed capacity research is permanent across prestige resets, permanently spends ruby, and makes that capacity buyable immediately in the run without waiting for the old level cap. Speed research still resets with the run.
