# Market Page

The Market is the room page immediately right of Research. Its internal page id is still `shop` for save and navigation compatibility.

It has `npc market`, `player market`, and `crystals` tabs. The `crystals` tab is third and shows real-money crystal bundle prices with bundle and price columns only. Pressing a price shows the support-unavailable popup. Payment and crystal grant behavior should stay out of this page until that flow is explicitly requested.

The `npc market` tab contains automatic NPC demand sales and shared NPC stock. The `player market` tab contains local player requests and player-to-player listings.
