# Workshop Page

The Workshop is the first room view.

It sits between Garden and Research. It shows one `elara's request` at a time, plus the summon action, bag popup, transient reward flyouts, discoveries, leaderboard, alliance controls, and page name. Segmented request progress and completion flights live in the shared top panel alongside mana, coin, crystal, and level. The first-run reveal gate keeps that progress row hidden until Elara introduces requests. Prestige is its own gated page after level 7. Workshop secondary actions unlock by player level; keep those gates in page managers and keep gameplay effects behind gameplay/backend facades.

Follow `docs/style.md`: high-contrast text, quiet surfaces, readable Lexend text, simple non-dialog boxes, and no decorative visuals. Reuse shared row, popup, border-label, and expandable-box patterns from `docs/ui-patterns.md`.
