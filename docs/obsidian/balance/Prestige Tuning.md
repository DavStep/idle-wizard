# Prestige Tuning

This is the balance reference for [[mechanics/Prestige|Prestige]]. Player-facing
details are split into [[mechanics/Prestige Milestones|Milestones & Rubies]] and
[[mechanics/Prestige Stars|Prestige Stars]].

## Key Values

- A milestone appears every 10 levels.
- Most milestones grant 1 ruby; level 50 grants 2 and level 100 grants 5.
- A prestige run restarts at half the claimed milestone level, with a minimum
  start level of 5.
