# Prestige Tuning

Prestige records completed level milestones and resets the run. A new run starts
at half the completed prestige milestone level; a level 10 reset starts at paid
player level 5.

## Rewards

Milestones are every 10 levels. The default reward is 1 ruby, with level 50
granting 2 ruby and level 100 granting 5 ruby.

Claiming a milestone also credits every lower unclaimed milestone through that
level.

## Unlock Lanes

- Prestige 1: advanced capacity studies.
- Prestige 3: UI-only run focus selector.
- Prestige 4: improved auto summon reserve controls.
- Prestige 5: stronger room study levels 6-10.
