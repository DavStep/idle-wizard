# Capacity Gates

Player level controls what the player may buy; it does not grant the capacity
for free.

## Early Caps

- Level 1: 2 garden tiles, 1 cauldron, no market stands.
- Level 2: 3 garden tiles.
- Level 4: first NPC and player market stands.
- Level 5: 5 garden tiles, 2 cauldrons, 2 NPC stands, 2 player stands.
- Level 10+: later market stand caps.

## Advanced Capacity

Ruby capacity research can unlock garden plots 6-12 and cauldrons 3-5 after
enough completed prestiges. Once complete, that capacity is buyable immediately
in the current run.
