# Capacity Gates

Progression raises what the player **may buy**; it does not grant the slot.

## Source-default caps

| Player level | Garden plots | Cauldrons | Trader stands | Player stands |
| ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: |
| 1 | 2 | 1 | 0 | 0 |
| 2 | 3 | 1 | 0 | 0 |
| 4 | 3 | 1 | 1 | 1 |
| 5 | 5 | 2 | 2 | 2 |
| 10 | 5 | 2 | 3 | 3 |
| 13 | 5 | 2 | 4 | 4 |
| 17 | 5 | 2 | 5 | 5 |

## Advanced capacity

**Emerald** capacity research unlocks Garden plots 6–12 and cauldrons 3–5 after enough completed Prestiges. Capacity research is permanent, but each slot still requires its coin purchase.
