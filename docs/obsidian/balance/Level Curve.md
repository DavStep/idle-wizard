# Level Curve

Tasks turn regular gameplay actions into level progress. A level advances only
after all requirements are complete and the player pays the completion coin cost.

## Early Hand-Authored Beats

- Level 1: summon and turn in sage seeds.
- Level 2: summon, sell, and turn in sage seeds.
- Level 3: research mint seed, summon mint seed, turn in mint seed.
- Level 4: grow and turn in sage and mint herbs.
- Level 5: research, brew, and turn in mana tonic.

## Long Curve Shape

Levels 5-10 bridge into real play. Later decades repeat the pattern:

```text
relief -> steady build -> hard level 9 -> wall level 10
```
