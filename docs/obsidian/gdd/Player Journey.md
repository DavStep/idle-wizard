# Player Journey

## First Session Spine

1. Start in [[Room Map|Workshop]].
2. Use mana to summon sage seeds.
3. Turn seeds into early level progress.
4. Use the Market to earn coin.
5. Research mint seed.
6. Use Garden to grow herbs.
7. Research and brew mana tonic.
8. Reach the first harder checkpoints and eventually prestige.

## Progression Feel

Levels 1-4 are hand-authored tutorial pacing around first summon, Market,
Research, and Garden beats. Levels 5-10 bridge into real play. Later decades use
a relief/build/checkpoint/wall rhythm.

## Player-Facing Promise

The player should always see one useful next action: produce a resource, unlock
a related tool, convert output into progress, or buy capacity that makes the
loop less cramped.
