# Plot Research

Plot progression is split into four independent lanes.

| Lane | What it changes | Currency | Prestige behavior |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| [[mechanics/garden/Plot Capacity Research|Capacity]] | Makes plots 6–12 buyable | emerald | permanent |
| [[mechanics/garden/Plot Automation Research|Automation]] | Auto-plant and auto-harvest per plot | ruby | resets |
| [[mechanics/garden/Plot Growth Research|Growth]] | Reduces growth time per plot | emerald | resets |
| [[mechanics/garden/Plot Levels|Yield level]] | Consumes and produces x2–x5 per cycle | crystal | resets |

Each [[mechanics/garden/Garden Plots|plot note]] carries the exact research IDs that target it.
